Who are Deftones

Deftones are an American alternative metal band from Sacramento, California, formed in 1988 They are known for blending heavy metal with elements of alternative rock, shoegaze, dream pop, and post-rock, creating a unique and atmospheric

Band Members

  • Chino Moreno – Vocals, sometimes guitar
  • Stephen Carpenter – Guitars
  • Abe Cunningham – Drums
  • Frank Delgado – Samples, keyboards, turntables
  • Chi Cheng – Former bassist (passed away in 2013)
  • Sergio Vega – Replaced Chi, later left

Musical Style

  1. Deftones' music stands out because it's unique
  2. Heavy but emotional
  3. Dreamy but aggressive
  4. Poetic but raw
  5. Chino Moreno’s voice ranges from soft whispering to intense screaming, making their music cinematic and surreal
